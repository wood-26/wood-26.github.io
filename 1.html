Shiro

Description

Shiro is a food that goes on a bread called injera. It has spices in it, although one might say it is not extremely spicy.

What You Will Need

    shiro powder
    water

Directions

    Firstly, fill the pan with water so that it is almost full.
    Secondly, heat up the pan on a stovetop on low heat.
    Thirdly, when the water is hot, put shiro powder in it.
    Fourthly, mix the shiro powder with the water while it is cooking.
    Fifthly, when it finishes cooking, it should behave mostly like a liquid. It could be a little thick. When that happens, that means you have finished making the shiro.
